Text Machine Lab

The Text Machine Lab at UMass Lowell conducts research in deep learning for natural language processing, with a focus on representation learning and model interpretability.

  • model interpretability and representation learning
  • efficient large model training
  • text-based temporal reasoning
  • conversational agents and argument mining
  • clinical NLP and predictive modeling
  • NLP for educational and digital humanities
